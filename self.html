“SELF DEFENCE”


Student Welfare Club, School of Civil Engineering
Date of the event
15th to 21st of March 2019
organised by
School of Civil Engineering
The School Of Civil Engineering organized “SELF DEFENCE EVENT” for the students of B.Tech, School of Civil Engineering on 15thof March, 2019.This event was organized by the students of 6th semester under the leadership of Asst. Prof. M. Sreenivasulu Reddy President and Asst. Prof. Bhavana.B Vice President , Student Welfare Club, School Of Civil Engineering.
The program started at 2:30PM and inaugurated by Dr.Y.Ramalinga Reddy, Chairman, Student Welfare Club and also Director, School Of Civil Engineering, Asst. Prof. M. Sreenivasulu Reddy and Asst.Prof. Bhavana. The guests for this program were Shihan Dr.Lakshmi, Shihan Ashwini and Renshi.M.Krishna .They trained the students along with their teams.
The program conducted in two sessions, the first session for day scholars from 2:30pm to 4:30pm and the second session for hostilities from 4:45pm to 5:45pm.

SITE VISIT TO UPCOMING GYMNASIUM, REVA CAMPUS


SCHOOL OF CIVIL ENGINEERING
Description of the evnt
Date of the event
20th March 2019
organised by
School of Civil Engineering .
School of Civil Engineering had arranged a field visit to upcoming Gym, REVA campus for students of 6th semester ‘B’ section, in relation with the subject Design and Drawing of RCC and Steel Structures (DDRCC&SS).
