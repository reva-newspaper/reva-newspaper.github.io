Technical talk on “How to write Research article and publish in Journal
Student development programme


School: Mechanical Engineering
Venue: Seminar Hall, Sir M V Block
Date: 25th February 2019
Description of EventTechnical talk on “How to write Research article and publish in Journal”.

The School of ECE had organized technical talk on “Engineering 5G Communication Systems” on 28th February 2019 between 3:00 pm to 4:00 pm in Aryabhatta Seminar hall, C V Raman Block. Dr. Nijas C M is a senior Application Engineer at ANSYS Inc. He is specialized in the domain of RF, Antenna, Radar, Signal Integrity, Power Integrity and Multiphysics simulation. He has been working with ANSYS from last 2 years. He is responsible for providing technical assistance for all RF, Antenna, Radar and 5G applications. He has been interacting with engineers from different industries which includes Automotive, Defense, RF and Power Electronics. Before he joined in ANSYS he was working as Research Consultant bin GE Global Research in Bangalore on the area of microwave material sensing for detecting different materials in the crude oils. Around 90 students from the School of ECE attended the Technical Talk.

Technical Talk was presented by Dr.Nithin and Dr. Shamanth
Students participated: 130
