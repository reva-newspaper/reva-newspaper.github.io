“Events conducted during 1st of March to 14th of March 2019

UDAAN 2K19”


About the Event
Events conducted during 1st of March to 14th of March 2019

Date:8th March 2019
Time: 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM

School:
Civil Engineering

Venue:
Formal program:​ Seminar Hall, Sir M V Block,1 st floor
Informal program: Open Air Theatre

Organisers of Event:
Student Welfare Club, School of Civil Engineering

The School of Civil Engineering organized a farewell for the students of B.Tech 8 th semester and M.Tech 4th semester on the 8 th of March, 2019. This event was organized by the students of 6 th semester under the leadership of Prof. M. Sreenivasulu Reddy President and Asst. Prof. Bhavana B Vice President. Student Welfare Club, School of Civil Engineerin the inaugural function took place in the Seminar Hall, Sir. M V Block, 1 st floor, from 12:00pm to 1:30pm. The occasion was graced by the guest, Registrar, Dr. M Dhanamjaya Sir who addressed the outgoing students. Dr. Y Ramalinga Reddy, Chairman, Student Welfare Club and also Director of School of Civil Engineering, inspired the students with his motivational speech. The student representatives of M.Tech and B.Tech students shared their experience at REVA University. Various Cultural events such as dance, music, skit, mime and games were organized from 3:00pm to 6:00pm at the OpenAir Theatre. Overall, the student volunteers of student welfare club managed to make the event a memorable one.

PARTICIPATION OF NATIONAL LEVEL WORKSHOP ON CIVIL BRIDGE DESIGNING


About the Event
PARTICIPATION OF NATIONAL LEVEL WORKSHOP ON CIVIL BRIDGE DESIGNING

Date: 10th March 2019
Time: 10:00 PM to 3:00 PM

School:
Civil Engineering

Venue:
Building Sciences Block, Alumni Ave, IITM, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

No. of Students attended the training course: 13

The Civil Engineering Association (CEA), IIT Madras organized National level Workshop on Civil Bridge Designing. 2nd year students and faculty members Mr. Raghunandan Koppad and Mr. Shivaprasad H of School of Civil Engineering, REVA UNIVERSITY participated in the workshop on theory, design and fabrication of Bridges and competed along with students from various other institutes and universities. One of our team of 5 members from REVA University won Runner-up place out of 36 teams which participated in the competition and received merit certificates from Anvira Edustation. All other participants received Certificates from CEA IIT MADRAS & Anvira Edustation. Dr. K. Ramamurthy, Head - Department of Civil Engineering, IITM specially appreciated all of our 13 students and 2 faculties of REVA University for participating in the workshop along with the more than 100 students participated from all parts of the Tamilnadu state. Dr. Y Ramalinga Reddy, Director, School of Civil Engineering, along with Mr. M. Sreenivasulu Reddy, ACE Coordinator, distributed the certificates to students at the school and motivated them to attend more such events to enlighten the future

SITE VISIT TO VOLVO CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT CAMPUS


About the Event
SITE VISIT TO VOLVO CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT CAMPUS

Date: 12th & 13th March 2019
Time: 8:30 PM to 3.00 PM

School:
School of Civil Engineering

Venue:
Volvo Pvt Ltd

School of Civil Engineering had arranged a field visit on 12th and 13th March 2019 to Volvo construction equipments campus, Jalahalli, for students of 8th semester, BTech and 2nd semester Mtech in relation with the subject Highway Geometric Design. This field visit provided an exposure to students in the field of various construction equipments used for road construction. Faculty members accompanied were Mrs Kanimozhee, Mr Pradeep Kumar B K and Mr. Avinash Rao
