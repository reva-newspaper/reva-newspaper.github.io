DIGITAAL: ECO FRIENDLY EXAMS


REVA University has made the roads of examination a digitalized one by getting associated with DigiTaal. The services provided by this company gives the students access internal assessment and examinations via tabs.

Paperless Examination System is one of the key components in the modern education environment. These kinds of systems could effectively reduce the workload of lecturers and improve work efficiency. However, almost all the existing systems available in the market are only designed for simple multiple choice questions, application type questions,etc. The students can select the options for multiple choice questions and they have space to write in our own handwriting and draw as well.

Every student fingerprint screen register along with their SRN number to have personalized login and logout. The tab based examinations provide high security to the answers, doesn't get deleted by any chance and it's safe. After the examinations if the students have any queries to be clarified they can ask their lecturers via tab itself.

Paperless system is a 21st century trend and should be embraced by our higher system of knowledge, the adoption of technology in information processing will make information faster, cheaper, increased audience, better retrieval of stored messages. It will not make the workforce jobless, productive-less, effortless and initiative-less.
