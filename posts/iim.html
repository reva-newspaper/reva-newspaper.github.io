REVA STUDENTS AT IIM KOZHIKODE


REVA University was represented by selected students from School of Management Studies in the B-Plan competiton organised by Make Intern in IIM Kozhikode on 1st February, 2019. The 4 day event started with an orientation on the 1st day followed by various competitions.

All the participating students were expected to presente their b-plan. REVA was represented by 2 teams who presented a b-plan on women's safety device and food that tastes like home. Despite the tough competition, our teams made it into the 2nd round. Despite not making it into the 3rd round, 2 students individually, won prizes in other competions like music and quiz. The overall experince was amazing and the students had a lot to learn. “Both the teams gave their best the team's gave all their efforts to win but the competition was tough out there” said a participant from REVA.
