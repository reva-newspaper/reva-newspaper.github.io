“Report on “Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp” conducted by UIIC”



About the Event
Report on “Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp” conducted by UIIC

Date: 6th to 8th March 2019
Time: 02:20 PM to 04:00 PM

School:
Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Venue:
C.V Raman Block, REVA University

Description of Event:
Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell of UIIC, REVA University successfully organized Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp-4 from 6th to 8th March 2019. The programme was organized in association with Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII). The camp was formally inaugurated on 6 th March 2019 by Mr. R V Tyagarajan, Company Secretary and Mr. Nicku Mathew Abraham, Project officer, EDII by lighting the lamp. The participants were welcomed by Dr. Kiran Kumari Patil, Director, UIIC, REVA University. Dr. Rajashekhar Biradar, Director, School of ECE gave the presidential talk and explained the significance of creating awareness about Entrepreneurship at educational institutes. The main objective of this Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp (EAC) is to create awareness among students of Engineering and Science courses about various facets of entrepreneurship as an alternative career option. The idea of organizing this workshop is to inculcate the fundamentals, key concepts of Entrepreneurship in the Science and Technology among students. The Three day camp concluded with a valedictory session and a humble vote of thanks. The main attractions of the camp were as follows:

  • Resource persons in the field of Innovation & Entrepreneurship were invited for expert lecture.
  • Practicing entrepreneur's success stories - common problems faced by entrepreneurs were discussed.
  • Participants visited REVA University’s Centers of Excellence as part of the camp.

About 80 students from School of ECE and School of EEE have successfully participated in the camp and each participant is awarded a certificate. The camp was successfully coordinated by Mr. Praveen H.J, Assistant Manager, UIIC, Mr. Purushothama, BDE, UIIC, Prof. Ravishankar, School of ECE, Prof. Mahesh School of EEE under the leadership and direction of Dr. Kiran Kumari Patil, Director, UIIC. UIIC team and EDC members of School of ECE also contributed towards conducting the event successfully.

Day 1: 6 th March 2019
Session 1 & Session 2: Mr. R V Tyagarajan Company Secretary, addressed participants and gave brief introduction to Entrepreneurship concepts in his first session. He also spoke about his experience of working closely with startups. He also cited the examples of successful entrepreneurs. The students were exposed to points on developing the passion towards entrepreneurship and also gained insights on the skills they need to develop to become an effective entrepreneur.
Session 3: Mr. Nicku Mathew, Project Officer, EDII addressed the participants and spoke about Social Entrepreneurship & made participants to work on Self-Rating Questionnaire. The session was highly interactive.
Session 4: Prof. Saji Varghese, Christ University, addressed the students He shared details about introduction to idea generation. His session was filled with fun learning components. He shared examples of Innovative products developed by using waste materials and also explained how cost effective solutions can be developed if we understand the way to diagnose the problem. Participants were surprised to know that some of the innovative products are developed by illiterates and young students like them.

Day 2: 7 th March 2019
The Second day of the camp was for Industry Visit. The Participants were taken for tour inside REVA University campus and they visited below places

  • Center of Excellence – Infrastructure Management Services, C.V Raman Block
  • REVA NEST – Technology Business Incubator, C.V Raman Block
  • Hi Tech Security Office – REVA University main gate
  • Sewage water treatment plant – Next to food court
  • Data Center – Platinum Green Building – Main Block
  • Roof top Solar Panel Installations – Platinum Green Building – Main Block

Students were explained in detail how REVA University has taken innovative steps to address the problems through technology. The Industry visit was coordinated by Mr. Purushothama.S, BDE, UIIC.

Day 3: 8 th March 2019
Session 1: Ms.Sandhra Jose, Project Coordinator, EDII conducted the Brain Storming Session. The participants were divided into groups and made them to list out products and services offered by companies (Domain specific). The team with maximum number of products and services listed was declared winner for the activity conducted. The session made the participants to discuss among team members.
Session 2: Ms.Nicku Mathew Project Officer EDII took session on Idea generation and conducted group activity. It had buddy sessions and peer learning activities to generate business ideas. Students presented their ideas as teams. Valuable feedback was given to each team to improve their business ideas.
Session 3: Mr.Kishor Jagirdar Chairman & Managing Director addressed the students Creativity and innovation, design thinking and grassroots innovation. The session was filled with energy filled activities to make participants understand the importance of creativity and innovation.
Session 4: Feedback was collected from students about the Entrepreneurship Awareness Camp followed by Valedictory of the camp. Dr. Rajashekhar Mandi, Director, School of EEE, REVA University gave the valedictory address to the particiapnts. The EAC concluded with distribution of Certificates to the participants followed by group photo session.
