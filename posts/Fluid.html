“Technical Talk on “Fluid Power Technology in Tipper Hydraulics””



About the Event
Technical Talk on “Fluid Power Technology in Tipper Hydraulics”

School:
Mechanical Engineering

Venue:
School Seminar Hall, School of Mechanical Engineering, Sir M V Block

Description of Event:
The School of Mechanical Engineering had organized Technical talk for 6th semester ‘E & F’ section students of Mechanical Engineering, REVA University on “Fluid Power Technology in Tipper Hydraulics” on Thursday, 7th March, 2019 between 011.00AM to 12.30PM. Around 120 students6th semester ‘E & F’ section and 4 faculty member of Mechanical Engineering are benefited from technical Talk, Mr. Sukumar Shetty K , Senior Engineer (R& D) WIPRO Infrastructure and Engineering, Bangalore, in association with Fluid Power Society of India.

Wipro Infrastructure Engineering is a multi-faceted and diverse industrial engineering business with expertise spanning over Hydraulics, Aerospace, Water treatment, Additive manufacturing and Automation Solutions.

The Hydraulics business with over 40 years of experience specializes in designing and manufacturing custom built Hydraulic Cylinders (Double Acting, Single Acting and Telescopic Cylinders) and Actuators that find application in diverse segments such as Construction & Earthmoving, Material & Cargo Handling, Forestry, Farm & Agriculture, Mining and Truck Tipping. Deep engineering expertise coupled with scalable manufacturing, capacity to innovate and relentless focus on Quality has made Wipro a leading hydraulic solution provider to global OEMs. With its 10 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and cross continental geographic presence across India, Europe, USA and Brazil, Wipro is one of the Largest Independent Hydraulic Cylinder Manufacturer in the World delivering over a million cylinders to OEMs annually.

Wipro Water, a division of Wipro Infrastructure Engineering offers end-to-end solutions in water and wastewater treatment for industrial applications. Wipro Water has capabilities to design, manufacture, install and maintain water and wastewater treatment plants for diverse industries including Oil & Gas, Steel, Pharma, Chemical and Beverages.

The Aerospace business of Wipro Infrastructure Engineering with manufacturing facilities in India, Israel and USA supplies Actuators, Structural and Precision Engineered Components to major Global OEMs for Aerospace applications.

Wipro 3D, a division of Wipro Infrastructure Engineering, is a leading provider of Additive Manufacturing services. Wipro's Advanced Manufacturing division brings a unique offering of Concept-to-Component services that include Design, prototyping and production for demanding applications in Aerospace, Space, Defense, Industrial, Oil & Gas and Healthcare Segments.

WIN Automation, a division of Wipro Infrastructure Engineering caters to the full-service line of Industrial Automation needs of Indian & ASEAN manufacturing industries. WIN Automation provides complete, end-to-end Industrial Automation Solutions that enable companies in the discrete manufacturing sector to be automated, digitized and smart.

Wipro Infrastructure Engineering partners with Kawasaki Precision Machinery, Japan to manufacture Hydraulic Pumps for Excavators, a niche and technologically advanced product.

Global workforce of over 2,000 have helped expand our capabilities and remain closer to our customers.

Mr. Sukumar Shetty K is working Senior Engineer (R& D) WIPRO Infrastructure and Engineering, Bangalore. He has vast experience in the field of Design and Development of Directional Control Valves, Pumps, Hydraulic Cylinders, System Design, Road Construction Equipment’s and 2 & 3 Wheeler service station equipment.

Talk focused on the following key points:

  1. Introduction to WIPRO Infrastructure and Engineering Products
  2. Basic of fluid power technology
  3. Design and working of Directional Control Valves
  4. Design and Construction of Gear and Piston Pumps
  5. Design and working of Telescopic double and single acting cylinders
  6. Micro Electro Mechanical System (MEMS) switch used to find the angle tipper hydraulics.

Following are the outcomes achieved with this Expert Talk:

  1. Define the basic definition of fluid power system and related other definitions like center of gravity, fluid, hydraulic actuator
  2. Identify and Design of the Directional control valves
  3. List the different types of pumps and control valves used in hydraulic system
  4. Know the working tipper with fluid power technology
  5. Identify the future in future in fluid power system and challenges in hydraulic system
  6. Ability to get idea of job opportunity in fluid power systems


Outcome of the Event:
Outcome is that M.Tech. Students gained knowledge on publishing a paper, writing proposal and IPR.
