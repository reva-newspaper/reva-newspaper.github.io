Patent Meeting


Report on Interaction with Patent Filing Agency

School: Mechanical Engineering Venue: School Library Date: 28/02/2019 Year: 2018-19

Description of the event:

An interaction session was arranged between senior faculty members of the school and Expert from Patent Filing Agency. Around 20 senior faculty members attended the session. Mr.Abhishek Tiwari from CPA Global, IP management system was the resource person.
The objective of the meet was to bring the awareness on the process and stages of patenting.
Mr.Abhishek explained about essential parameters to be considered for a patent to be applied. He told a patentable topic should have end utility and novelty. He further explained different stages of patenting like Provisional (Idea Stage), Non provisional, patent pending and patent granted stages.
He also explained about grading involved in patenting like X, Y and A grades. He informed about duration of various stages of patenting.
Mr.Abhishek further detailed about international patenting and its advantages. He explained about commercialization of patents and various costs incurred in filing patents. He answered all queries posed by faculty members.
Outcome of the event is that faculty members gained a knowledge on different stages of filing a patent and its importance.
