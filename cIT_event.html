“Events in School of C & IT”



Event:
CSI Founder’s day-2019
Venue:
Reva University

Description of Event:
The School of Computing and IT under RACIT Activity celebrated the " CSI Founder’s day-2019 ​ " for the
students of REVA University. The event was a collaborative work of Clubs of School of C&IT, namely CSI,
ACM, Ideation and Project. Students from various schools took an interest and displayed their thoughts
over the span of this event which comprised of 3 technical activities.

Event:
TALK ON “RESEARCH PAPER WRITING”
Venue:
Reva University

Description of Event:
This talk aimed at offering simple, concrete suggestions on how to improve writing skills leading to

writeup of a research paper for submissions to National and International Conferences conducted within

and outside REVA University.

Event:
IT QUIZ (Preliminary rounds of TCS TECH BITES )
Venue:
Reva University

Description of Event:
The students were asked to form a team of 2 members. They attempted a quiz set of 20 questions.
A total of 40 teams took part in the event. The top 10 teams were shortlisted for Bangalore region IT quiz
event conduction which is to be held at SJBIT on 21 March 2019.

The Director of the School, Dr. Sunil Kumar S. Manvi sir motivated students to Join CSI, IEEE and ACM as
student members. He gave inputs to the students that would lead them to form networks with Technical
leaders and shall endeavor them to Publish quality research papers, and apply for Copyrights and
Patents.
